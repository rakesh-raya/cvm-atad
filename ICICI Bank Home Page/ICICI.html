ICICI Bank announces extension of the moratorium for loans and credit facilities

In these extraordinary times of the Covid-19 pandemic, we at ICICI Bank are committed to stand by our customers to help them tide over the crisis. This is with reference to the extension of the moratorium (postponement of instalments or payments) for loans, credit cards and working capital facilities for another three months-from June 1,2020. Customers can now apply for the moratorium for June 2020. If they wish to avail it for July and August 2020, they will need to apply again when the Bank notifies them.
